Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get test results?

+

Most lab tests are processed within 24 hours. Imaging results are typically available within 48 hours.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

+

Some tests require a referral, while others (like basic blood tests) can be booked directly.

Are your diagnostic tests covered by insurance?

+

We accept most major insurance providers. Please check with your insurer for coverage details.

How do I prepare for a specific test?

+

Preparation instructions vary by test. Our team will provide detailed guidance upon booking your appointment.

Can I get a copy of my test results?

+

Yes, you can request a copy of your results. We offer secure online portals and printed reports.

What are your operating hours?

+

Our centers are open Monday to Saturday, from 8 AM to 7 PM. Please check our website for specific location hours.

Is online appointment booking available?

+

Yes, you can easily book appointments through our website or mobile app 24/7.

Do you offer home sample collection?

+

For certain tests, we offer convenient home sample collection services. Please contact us for more details.
